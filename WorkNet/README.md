#  WorkNet – Employment & Skills Platform

##  Project Overview
WorkNet is a UX/UI design concept for a digital platform aimed at addressing unemployment and job accessibility in South Africa. The platform connects job seekers with opportunities, skills development resources, and career pathways using a user-friendly digital experience.

---

##  Problem Statement
Many young people in South Africa struggle to find employment due to:
- Lack of access to job opportunities
- Skills mismatch in the job market
- Limited exposure to digital career platforms

---

##  Proposed Solution
WorkNet is designed to:
- Match users with suitable job opportunities
- Provide access to online learning and skill development
- Help users build verified digital profiles
- Improve accessibility to employment resources
